# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Construction professionals, contractors, equipment buyers, and procurement managers worldwide. They work on infrastructure, building, and industrial projects and need to source reliable concrete pumps, mixers, and related machinery. Their context is high-stakes procurement: evaluating specs, comparing suppliers, verifying track record, and ultimately requesting a quote or making a purchase decision. They visit the site from desktop during work hours (primary) and mobile on-site (secondary).

## Product Purpose

KEWAN's website exists to showcase construction machinery, demonstrate global operational reliability through real case studies and technical depth, and generate qualified sales leads (quote requests, WhatsApp inquiries). Success looks like a visitor landing from search or referral, assessing KEWAN's equipment and credibility across one or two sessions, then initiating contact.

## Brand Personality

Professional · Reliable · Global

Voice is authoritative but approachable — the confidence of an established industrial partner without jargon or cold formality. Content is thorough and concrete: specs, numbers, real projects, real teams. The tone communicates competence earned through delivery, not marketing claims.

## Anti-references

- **Cheap / rough template sites.** No low-effort layouts, low-res imagery, broken spacing, or unfinished sections. Every page must feel deliberately built, not scaffolded.
- **Overly designed SaaS / startup aesthetics.** This is industrial B2B — no gradient text, glassmorphism, floating CTA bars, numbered section eyebrows, or hero-metric templates.
- **Outdated 2000s industrial sites.** No tiny clip-art icons, table-based layouts, garish gradients, or stock-photo-heavy hero sections.

## Design Principles

1. **Trust through precision.** Construction equipment buyers make high-stakes decisions. Every pixel conveys competence: clean layouts, exact spec data, no ambiguity. The site earns trust by feeling as reliable as the machinery.

2. **Substance over flash.** Hero imagery of actual equipment (not stock). Real case studies with concrete details. Technical specs that invite comparison. Decoration always serves content; content is never placeholder.

3. **Depth on demand.** The homepage is the landing; product pages are where decisions happen. Navigation, specs, FAQs, and data are accessible but not overwhelming — progressive disclosure from hero → category → product detail → quote.

4. **Global reach, local relevance.** KEWAN operates in the Philippines, Indonesia, Egypt, and beyond. The design shows real places, real teams, real projects — not generic "global" imagery.

5. **Bold but not brash.** Industrial strength without aggression. The brand blue is commanding but not cold; the overall feel inspires confidence, not intimidation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG 2.1 AA as baseline (body text ≥4.5:1 contrast, non-text elements ≥3:1).
- Forms must have visible labels, clear error states, and keyboard-operable submission.
- All animations must respect `prefers-reduced-motion` (crossfade or instant as fallback).
- Consider color-blind accessibility for any status indicators or charts.
